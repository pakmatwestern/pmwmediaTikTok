🎥 TikTok Sales Best Practices

✅ DO'S:

Video Quality: Clear lighting, stable shot, show product details
Timing: Upload 7-9PM for maximum engagement
Hashtags: Use trending + product-specific tags
Engagement: Reply comments dalam 1-2 jam

❌ DON'TS:

Free Delivery: NEVER enable - akan rugi profit
Overprice: Jangan set harga too high dari competitor
Ignore Comments: Slow response = lost sales
Poor Content: Blurry video, bad lighting kills conversion

🎯 Golden Rule:

Consistency beats perfection - post daily dengan quality standard yang consistent
